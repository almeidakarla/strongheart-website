Investing Partners

We strive to provide our investors with complete transparency through honest and open reporting. To that end, StrongHeart Energy offers online access to investor accounts. Once you have conducted business with StrongHeart Energy or your firm has permitted us to open an account, instructions to log in to the secure section of our website will be automatically emailed to you. With this access, you can download your investment history as well as K-1 documents.

In addition, we post updates on the activities in our energy funds, webinar replays, video clips, and industry-related news articles to our website.

StrongHeart Energy is a value-added partner for Broker-Dealers, Registered Representatives, Family Offices, and RIAs. We offer education, direct client services, and an insider’s perspective on the energy market. As a leader in designing and structuring direct oil and gas investments, our firm provides various investment options, each with unique income and tax planning solutions to help your practice attract, grow, and retain high-net-worth clients.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is your typical project structure?

    In a typical project, institutional investors invest alongside StrongHeart Energy and other joint venture partners. This approach offers improved access to a wide range of projects with customizable deal terms to meet the income, growth, tax, and investment horizon needs of institutional investors.

  • What strategy does StrongHeart Energy employ when making investment decisions?

    First and foremost, the return profiles of the assets must align with the objectives we have set for our partners.

    Additionally, we seek drilling areas with low geologic and operational risk. In recent years, StrongHeart Energy has adopted a 'big-data' mindset, enabling us to limit risk associated with investment opportunities for institutional partners.

  • Who can invest in a StrongHeart Energy offering?

    StrongHeart Energy’s limited partnerships are available to accredited investors only. For the definition of an accredited investor, please learn more here.

  • Do you work with Registered Reps and RIAs?

    StrongHeart Energy works with Registered Representatives of Broker-Dealers who have obtained their Series 7 or 22 licenses, along with the appropriate state registrations.

    Registered Investment Advisors (RIAs) and Investment Advisor Representatives (IARs) must be registered with their state or with the SEC, if applicable.

    CPAs and other financial and accounting professionals are encouraged to contact us using the form below for information on availability.

  • How can I start investing?

    To start investing, please, get in touch through the form below or through the Get In Touch page.

  • How do you see StrongHeart Energy evolving over the next five years? Does the future involve more strategic relationships with world-class operators as well as operated drilling activities?

    StrongHeart Energy intends to remain active in all aspects of the Exploration and Production (E&P) market. The firm is continually exploring new operated developmental opportunities as well as partnerships with other operators in core areas. Our strong financial position enables us to rigorously evaluate each opportunity and enter into projects and partnerships that offer the best returns for our investors.
