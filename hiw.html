Investing in Oil and Gas with StrongHeart Energy


Investing in oil and gas with StrongHeart Energy is a streamlined and transparent process, designed to provide you with the information and tools you need to make informed investment decisions. Here are the three main steps to get started:


01

Explore Our Offerings

Begin by visiting the Current Operations page on our website. This section provides detailed information about the wells and projects we are currently offering for investment. You will find comprehensive data on each well, including its location, production statistics, and projected returns. This information will help you evaluate the potential of each investment opportunity.
02

Create an Account or Log In

Once you have identified a project that interests you, the next step is to create an account on our website. If you already have an account, simply log in. Creating an account is straightforward and provides you with access to a wealth of resources and tools. With an account, you can manage your investments, track performance, and stay updated on new opportunities.
03

Review and Sign the PPM

After logging in, you will have access to the Private Placement Memorandum (PPM) for the investment opportunity you are interested in. The PPM contains all the critical details about the investment, including terms and conditions. It is essential to read this document thoroughly to understand the specifics of the investment.
04

Invest

Once you have reviewed the PPM and are ready to proceed, you can complete the purchase of the investment unit directly through our website. Alternatively, you can work with one of our financial advisor partners to facilitate the investment. The purchase process is designed to be secure and efficient, ensuring that your investment is processed smoothly.
