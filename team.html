About Us

  1. StrongHeart Energy
  2. About Us

Robust and reliable processes that result in performance.


StrongHeart Energy specializes in providing direct investment into non-operational wellbore interests in the United States, focusing on opportunities near the drilling or completion stages of the wellbore lifecycle.

Our investment strategy is meticulously crafted to target wellbores with proven success, particularly those adjacent to offset wells that have yielded exceptional returns for stakeholders.

We prioritize wellbores that offer substantial potential and minimal risk, exclusively allocating investments to fields with verified, extensive oil and gas reserves.

About Us

Pillars

Mission

StrongHeart Energy offers direct investment into high-potential, low-risk oil well projects near the drilling or completion stages of the well’s lifecycle, in established oil plays across the U.S.

Operating Partners

StrongHeart Energy partners with leading and established U.S. operators, to ensure that we align ourselves and our investing partners with the interests of the U.S.’s most reputable companies.

Deal Flow

StrongHeart Energy goes to great lengths to establish strategic relationships and deep industry connections in order to provide our investing partners with access to hardto-find and high-quality investment opportunities.

Technology

Strongheart Energy works with operators who leverage the most advanced technologies in the Oil and Gas industry to make scientific, precise, and profitable investments.

About Us

Strategy

Our strategy stems from our direct relationships with some of the most prolific drillers and operators in the USA. These large operators, referred to as the “Majors”, and other mid-size operators, look for capital partners to help them derisk and lower their exposure to any one drilling project.

In addition, over the past 10 years, and as a result of ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) incentives and mandates, the O&G space has seen a dramatic exodus of capital from institutional banks and private equity firms. Consequently, StrongHeart has even richer opportunities to participate in drilling programs alongside the most professional, technically savvy, and well-funded drillers and operators in the country.
